Eggnog Crème Brûlée

Ingredients 8 cups water 2/3 cup Best Eggnog 3/4 cup heavy cream 1/2 cup whole milk 1/8 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg 5 large egg yolks 1/3 cup plus 1/4 cup granulated sugar Instructions Heat the oven to 300°F and arrange a rack in the middle. Bring the water to a simmer in a medium saucepan over high heat; keep at a simmer. Combine the eggnog, cream, milk, and nutmeg in a medium saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
